Vision:

VISION: In support to the college mission, The library service will provide information resources and service in cooperation with the academic departments and faculty with clear focus on student learning as well as in the community where people and ideas intereact to expand learning and to facilitate the creation of new knowledge.

Mission:

MISSION: The CPC Library Services serves as the repository material of both print and non-print informational resources to provide service and facilities which support the different programs of the college. It also contributes to the academic success, growth and development of students through teaching and by promoting information literacy.

Objectives:
